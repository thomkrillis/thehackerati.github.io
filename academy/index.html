Audience

Hackerati Academy was conceived and created both for the training of entry-level employees and for the continuous education of experienced employees.

General prerequisites for all courses include a solid understanding of fundamental engineering skills, mastery of at least one programming language (we don't care which one), a propensity for solving difficult problems, and general fearlessness.

Our team includes seasoned practitioners and instructors from leading university engineering programs.

Pedagogy

Academy courses cover advanced topics in agile development, the latest technologies, and the most effective UI/UX practices for a variety of devices and platforms. Every course is project-based and team-oriented, just like real-world agile development efforts. The focus of the course work is the application of individually acquired technical knowledge on a professional project team, rather than the acquisition of technical knowledge itself. Each course has specific prerequisites and resources for individual study.

Our training methodologies reflect what we do every day at work. Our courses incorporate writing stories, planning sprints, holding daily standups, practicing Test-Driven Development, utilizing Continuous Integration, and holding retrospectives.
